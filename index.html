About Me

I come to design from an academic program steeped in engineering, design studies, psychology, and a slew of other fields. That experience crossed with in-depth knowledge of the technology industry today makes me obsessive about user experience across the board. I'm as likely to examine the frame rate and backend of a product as I am the font choices and button placement. I'm currently at Startup Institute Chicago studying Product and Design.

I'm interested in user experience design, graphic design, and photography.

Web and Experience Design

Summon Wireframe and Aesthetic Mockup

At Startup Institute I was on a team that went through the design process for Summon, an app meant to facilitate groups of friends hanging out in real life. Above is a wireframe that was made based on user research and the aesthetic mockup that resulted from that wireframe.

Summon User Flow

While mapping the user flow for Summon we began at an abstract level with what kinds of questions we would ask and how we could facilitate people getting through to the end goal of the application as quickly as possible. This flow shows the start and the steps needed to get someone to a final recommendation. After a lot of work we managed to get the user to their end goal in three screens.

EMR Survey Wireframe

In designing a survey for a large EMR company I had to think about the various displays on which I could only show one survey, that restriction lead to me limiting the information shown at any one time with some autoscrolling to link between questions. Above is the initial wireframes.

Graphic Design

The WUD Social Media Strategy Guide

This piece was the cover to the Social Media Strategy Guide made in 2011 for the Wisconsin Union. My team had defined the branding aesthetic for the Wisconsin Union Directorate and here both enforced that aesthetic as well as giving guidance to the committees on how to best use social media.

Map for the Revelry Music and Arts Festival

At the Revelry Music Festival in 2013 I was tasked with making a map of the grounds. I needed to make it informative so that people could find thier way around but still maintain the branding guidelines we had previously developed for the festival.

Teaser poster for the Revelry Music and Arts Festival

This was our first teaser poster for Revelry. It was intended to introduce people to the brand and to get the word out about the festival.
